RaidFit Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

RaidFit is a local-first fitness game. Your training data lives on your device, not on our servers.

What stays on your phone

Everything you generate in the app — workouts, sets, body-region progress, hunter profile, gear, settings — is stored only on your device. We run no account system and no analytics, and we cannot see any of it. Deleting the app deletes the data (use your device's backup if you want to keep it).

What is processed off-device

Purchases only. Subscriptions are processed by Google Play and managed through RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc.), which acts as our purchase infrastructure. To validate your license, RevenueCat receives:

  • Purchase history (the subscription/product you bought)
  • A random app-scoped device identifier and basic device metadata

RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.
We never receive your name, email, or payment details; Google Play handles payment.

What we don't do

  • No ads, no ad identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No selling or sharing of data
  • No accounts, no passwords

Children

RaidFit is intended for users 18 and older.

Changes

If a future version adds online features (e.g. leaderboards), this policy will be updated first and the app will ask before syncing anything.
